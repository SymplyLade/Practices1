About Our Library

Our library management system provides easy access to our collection of books. Students can browse and search for books, while librarians have additional privileges to manage the collection.

Features:

  • Browse and search books (All users)
  • View book details and availability
  • Add, update, and delete books (Librarians only)
  • Manage book status (Available/Checked Out)
